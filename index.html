Superhero Builder – Training Core
Hypertrophy-based split with strength elements to drive long-term growth.
Program

Built around heavy compounds followed by high-quality isolation work, with multiple variations to keep progress moving while hammering the same core goals.

Base Split (repeat):
  • Day 1 – Chest + Triceps
  • Day 2 – Back + Rear Delts
  • Day 3 – Shoulders + Biceps
  • Day 4 – Legs
  • Day 5 – Chest + Triceps (Strength focus)
  • Day 6 – Back + Rear Delts
  • Day 7 – Shoulders + Biceps
  • Day 8 – Legs (Strength focus)
Intensity First Every working set is taken close to or past failure. Rest 2–3 minutes so quality stays high, not rushed junk volume.
Recovery Rules High-protein diet, 8 hours of sleep, and at least 1–2 rest days per week when the next target muscle group is still sore.
Structure Compounds lead each day (bench, squats, rows, presses), followed by isolation for chest, triceps, delts, back, and legs to finish the muscle.
Flexibility Machine movements have dumbbell / cable substitutions so the program travels with you to any halfway-decent gym.
Non-negotiables
  • High protein intake + slight surplus when gaining.
  • Train near failure every set — no lazy reps.
  • Respect joints and injuries; swap moves instead of forcing pain.
  • Track patterns: skipped sessions, poor sleep, sloppy food.
